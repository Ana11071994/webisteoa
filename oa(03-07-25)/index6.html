Object Automation, a California-based leader in AI solutions and chip design services, organized the Global AI Festival and Future Conference at SRM Ramapuram Campus, Chennai, on August 17, 2024. This prestigious event brought together AI-enthusiastic students to explore the future of artificial intelligence and collaborate on cutting-edge innovations. Featuring keynote speeches and discussions, the festival addressed AI's transformative role in sectors such as healthcare and aerospace, as well as the ethical considerations and societal impacts of AI technologies. A highlight of the event was the Hackathon, a dynamic competition where student teams tackled real-world challenges using AI. The winning teams were celebrated for their innovative solutions and awarded prizes, including mentorship opportunities and access to state-of-the-art AI tools. The festival underscored Object Automation's commitment to driving innovation and collaboration within the global AI community, showcasing the immense potential of collective efforts to shape the future of artificial intelligence.
