I was honored to receive a special invitation to speak with faculty and researchers at Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology (RIT) and deliver a presentation to students on the future of emerging technologies. During my three-hour session on August 14, 2024, I highlighted key advancements in chip architecture, such as 3D chip stacking and neuromorphic computing, which are crucial for driving progress in AI and other advanced technologies. I also explored the transformative potential of quantum computing, demonstrating how quantum bits can revolutionize computational power to tackle complex challenges in fields like financial modeling, drug development, and climate prediction. In today’s rapidly evolving tech landscape, staying ahead in AI, ChipDesign, and QuantumComputing is essential for organizations striving to maintain a competitive edge. As these technologies continue to evolve, their convergence will unlock groundbreaking innovations and provide solutions to some of the world’s most complex problems, making it more critical than ever for companies to stay at the forefront of these advancements.
