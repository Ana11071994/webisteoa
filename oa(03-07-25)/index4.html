Organized a three-hour AI workshop at my school, Kellett Higher Secondary School at Chennai, for one hundred high school kids (where I studied from 6th to 12th grade). The workshop explored the foundations of artificial intelligence, including its history, essential ideas, and real-world applications in a range of sectors. The attendees' active participation enhanced the program and added to the lively conversation on AI technologies, ethics, and social implications. At the end of the workshop, students took a competitive quiz to test their understanding, and the best scorers received awards. Aluminous's and classmates' participation was essential to the workshop's success. Thanks to the School's headmaster as well as all other faculties who made the event more successful.
