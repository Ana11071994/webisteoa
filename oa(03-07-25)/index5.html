ObjectAutomation hosted a tech meet on 10th August 2024 where various leaders from industry , academic experts, and government representatives got together and discussed about emerging technologies such as AI, Chip Design, and Quantum Computing to drive innovation and collaboration. This event an eye opener platform to explore the latest advancements, shared research insights, and identitied challenges and opportunities within these rapidly evolving fields. Key objectives of the meet which included : Exploring Emerging Technologies: Present and discuss the latest developments in AI, Chip Design, and Quantum Computing, emphasizing their transformative impact on industries and society.

Fostering Collaboration: Encouraged partnerships between industry, academia, and government to accelerate research and development, and drive the commercialization of new technologies.

Promoting Talent Development: opportunities for internships and postdoctoral research positions in the US and India, aiming to bridge the gap between academic research and industry needs.

Government Role: Discussed the role of government policies, funding, and support in fostering innovation, and how public-private partnerships can enhance technological growth.

These tech meet will help to establish joint research initiatives, the creation of internship and postdoc programs, and the development of a collaborative ecosystem that supports the growth of critical technologies worldwide.
