About 5G and 6G Research

5G offers significant business opportunities across various industries, including healthcare, automotive, smart factories, and mission critical communications. Its advanced features, flexibility, virtualization, and programmability support the possibility of diverging architectures for new services.

6G (On Research ) offers faster speeds and bandwidth, ultra-low latency, enhanced connectivity, improved reliability, and AI integration. It supports a larger number of connected devices, enhances IoT ecosystem, and offers real-time holographic communication and AR/VR experiences. Applications include smart cities, autonomous vehicles, healthcare, education, and industrial automation. It also offers real-time interaction and high reliability for remote surgeries and healthcare.
