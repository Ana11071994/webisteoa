Our Offerings

Our organization offers a comprehensive range of services and solutions across various sectors. Here’s a breakdown of the tech offerings:

1. Technology Enablement and Centers of Excellence in Universities

  • Technical Courses: Offering in-person and on-campus training tailored to university curriculums.
  • Internships: Opportunities available at campus locations, company premises, or online.
  • Technical Workshops: Short-term workshops ranging from 1 to 5 days.
  • Global AI or CHIP Design Conference: We organize one-day international conferences on AI or chip design.
  • Hardware and Software Setup: Collaborating with universities to set up essential hardware and software for delivering courses and workshops.

2. Onshore/Offshore Development

  • AI Resources: Providing highly skilled AI professionals for both strategic and client-specific projects.
  • HPC Resources: Offering expertise in high-performance computing for strategic and client projects.
  • Chip Design and Verification: Skilled professionals focusing on chip design and verification for strategic and client initiatives.

3. Research Solutions

  • AI-based Solutions: Proposing AI solutions to governments or for industry-specific applications.
  • Chip Design Solutions: Developing and proposing chip design innovations for government or industry applications.

These initiatives not only strengthen academic and professional partnerships but also foster innovation and practical skill development in critical and emerging technological fields.

Dr Muralikrishna Iyyanki,
Chief Advisor


Dr Muralikrishna Iyyanki is a distinguished academic and consultant specializing in geospatial modeling, environment and health analytics. He holds a Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Science and an M.Tech from IIT Madras. As the Chief Mentor for Berkeley India Academic Programs, he focuses on Open Innovation and Smart Villages. He is also the Founder Editor of multiple journals, including Geospatial Today.Iyyanki has held leadership positions such as Chairman of ISPRS Working Group III/9 and has consulted for the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation. His research interests include design thinking, lean management, and hyperspectral remote sensing. A fellow of several prestigious organizations, he is committed to advancing technology and innovation for sustainable development.
